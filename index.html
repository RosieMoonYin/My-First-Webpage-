Tilopas 6 Words of Advice

  1. Dont recall.

    2. ~Let go of what has passed.

  2. Dont imagine.

    3. ~Let go of what may come.

  3. Dont think.

    4. ~Let go what is happening now.

  4. Dont examine.

    5. ~Dont try to figure anything out.

  5. Dont control.

    6. ~Dont try to make anything happen.

  6. REST.

    7. ~Relax, right now, and REST.
